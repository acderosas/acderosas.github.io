About Me (Flips sides with Photos with photos as inside book cover) pg 1

My history with computers starts with me in Middle School using a VAIO Desktop computer. Just playing flash games on Armor Games and doing general web surfing. During high school I discovered what computer science was and took it as an elective. The class went over the basics: HTML, Scratch, binary, coded a toy car to move, and much more! Having been undecided on my future, I remembered back to High School and decided computer science was for me. I went to Folsom Lake College to get my GE's and learn more about programming: C++, Assembly, Calculus, data structures, and so on. Transferring to California State University of Chico, I expanded my knowledge on shell programming, data structures, databases, frameworks, ML through Tensorflow, use different tools like Wireshark, and went more into different languages (C, Python, and more JS).
